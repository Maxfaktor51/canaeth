To the seeker of truths lost and tales forgotten,

I dare present to you the Scriptorium Canaethiae - my life's work, my burden, and my gift to the world. A sanctuary for the old spirit of our land, this chronicle is the echo of Canaeth’s soul, captured in ink and bound in leather—a testament to its undying legacy.

Herein lies the saga of our people, from the heights of triumph to the depths of tragedy. As a humble bard and chronicler, I traversed Canaeth to gather these tales, ensuring our history would not be swept away by the winds of oblivion.

The Scriptorium stands as a beacon for the brave. May you find within these pages the wisdom of kings, the valor of knights, and the whispers of prophets.

In eternal service to the truth and the legacy of Canaeth,

- Master Eadric, the Chronicler
